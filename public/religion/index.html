Religion

When the founders created Alpha Culture they wanted to make it a unviversal culture. Something that everyone could understand and agree upon. Because the culture was targeting everyone, the founders decided that they couldn't get everyone to simpily agree upon one culture and so the Founders decided that they would become Non-Theistic. However strong beliefs about freedom, unity, and development were enforced. These core beliefs were what made Alpha Culture not only so unique, but independent from other cultures. The religion itself was like a rule you had to follow in order to be accepted as an Alphaninan. These beliefs / religion were later known as Alphanitian.
