Religion

When the founders created Alpha Culture they wanted to make it a universal culture. Something that everyone could understand and agree upon. Because the culture was targeting everyone, the founders decided that they couldn't get everyone to simply agree upon one culture and so the Founders decided that they would become Non-Theistic. However strong beliefs about freedom, unity, and development were enforced. These core beliefs were what made Alpha Culture not only so unique, but independent from other cultures. The religion itself was like a rule you had to follow in order to be accepted as an Alphaninan. These beliefs / religion were later known as Alphanitian.

Ritual: In order for the Alphanians to prove their loyalty, Alphanians are expected to help others in need. This can be done in any way, however a common tradition is to have the entire family participate in The Solution.
