National Symbol

The Alpha Culture represents unity and freedom. Because Alpha Culture doesn't have a specific physical location, and instead spread thought nations and the world, Alpha Culture uses the Earth as its national symbol. The lines connecting continents represent the unity that Alphanians believe in and the space surrounding the Earth represents the freedom which everyone is entitled to.
