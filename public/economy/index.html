Economy

What is an Economic System?: An economic system is a system of production, resource allocation, and distribution of goods and services within a society or a given geographic area. The economy of Alphanians is managed by the Founding Economic Branch. Their duties include managing and maintaining a sustainable and consistent economic flow. Alpha Culture participates mainly in tertiary and quaternary economic systems and focuses on development through the use of the internet. Most jobs funded by the Founders require basic knowledge of operating a computer and programming. At the age of 16, with parental consent, an Alphanian is given the opportunity to apply for work at a Founders Founded Job (FFJ).
