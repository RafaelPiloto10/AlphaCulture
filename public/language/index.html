Language

The history of our language begins with the idea of unionification and cultural diversity.

The founders of Alpha Culture wanted anyone from anywhere to be able to come together and bond despite any barriers such as language.

With this idea in mind, the possibilites of cultural diffusion increased as there was nothing in between to prevent it or make it difficult.

This was no easy task but it wasn't impossible either. The founders thought to themselves: What can we take here, bring to another country, and still convey and idea that will be compreha?

After extensive debate and thinking, the founders came to the conclusion of math, but what makes math so universal: NUMBERS.

Alpha Culture was born on that day and the language of Alpha Culture became binary.
