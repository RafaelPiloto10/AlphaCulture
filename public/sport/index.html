The Solution

The Solution is a global wide event where anyone can come together and focus on making the world a better place. The founders will come up with a list of 5 problems which is currently or will eventually harm earth or its citizens. At this event billions of minds will be put together to solve any of the 5 problems of their choosing. Once a person / team believes that they have a solution it will be presented to the founders where they will run tests and simulations. Should it prove to be successful those who contributed will be honored in the "Hall of Fame" and respected by all. They will be given a wealthy amount of money and a reasonable payment for their efforts.

Note: The 5 challanges presented on Solution day are available after the day if they are not solved and will continue to be available throughout the year. This is the culture's traditional passing time and considered the equivalent as "serving for your country".
