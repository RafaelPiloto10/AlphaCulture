Education

When the Founders created Alpha Culture they were all fond of free and complete education. Because of this they created a new and equal education system, which offered anyone and everyone the ability to receive a complete education in any degree for free! Alphanian education focuses on Cyber education and Computer Science. Alpha Culture believes in development through the use of science and because the internet is our future, they prepare students by giving them all the prerequisites for the future. In Pre-K students learn basic addition and subtraction and by the time the graduate High School students will be advanced in Networking, Programming, and Engineering. These will be mandatory courses like morse code and math throughout a student's scholastic years. This is not to say that everyone will be an IT personnel (No offense IT personnel), but instead offer students a technological and innovative perspective and skills in order to better help solve their problems.
