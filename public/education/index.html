Education

When the Founders created Alpha Culture they were all fond of free and complete edcuation. Because of this they created a new and equal education system, which offered anyone and everyone the ability to receieve a complete education in any degree for free! Alphanian education focuses on Cyber education and Computer Science. Alpha Culture believes in development through the use of science and because the internet is our future, they prepare students by giving them all the prerequisets for the future. In Pre-K students learn basic addition and subtraction and by the time the graduate High School students will be advanced in Networking, Programming, and Engineering. These will be maditory courses like english and math through out a student's scholastic years. This is not to say that everyone will be an IT person, but instead offer students what they will need in order to survive in the future. As a matter of fact, diverse electives and job opportunites are offered throughout the world and available to anyone.
