Politics

What is a Political System?: political system is a set of principles, laws, ideas, and procedures all of which form its government.

The only form of government in Alpha Culture is its Founders. The Founders aren't leaders of a country but instead leaders of believers. They are the political system for group of people who have the same beliefs. This is especially convenient because Alpha Culture isn't in a physical location but instead spread throughout earth. The Founders are split into 3 branches (3 founders per branch): The Economic Branch, The Educational Branch, and the Political Branch. Each branch specializes in making all of the rules, which is later voted upon by the Alphanians. If a majority of the population votes for an idea or law it will be passed and made official. Alphanians have the freedom of voting anyone to be a founder at the end of every year (December 31st) as well as voting to kick any one out. The Founders are made up of a group of 9 Alphanians of any race or gender. If the people vote for it, they will get what they want. That is to say that if everyone voted to only eat candy for the rest of the year, they would indeed eat candy all year. Alphanians must be 18 years old to vote as it is proven to be the age of maturity.
